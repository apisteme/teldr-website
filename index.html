The Problem We Solve

Manually analyzing video and audio files is a tedious, time-consuming, and error-prone process. Whether it's reviewing hours of surveillance footage, transcribing lengthy interviews, or finding specific moments in a podcast, the effort required can be immense, leading to missed insights and inefficiencies.

There's a critical need for intelligent tools that can automatically understand, summarize, and make media content easily searchable, freeing up valuable human resources for more strategic tasks.

Our Solution: Teldr.ai

Teldr.ai revolutionizes how you interact with media. Simply upload your audio or video, and our advanced AI goes to work, providing you with a comprehensive, actionable breakdown of its content.

  • Automatic Summaries: Get concise overviews of long media.
  • Event Timelines: Pinpoint key moments with precise timestamps.
  • Full Transcripts: Accurate speech-to-text for every word spoken.
  • Visual Thumbnails: Navigate content visually with ease.
  • Natural Language Q&A: Ask questions and get context-aware answers instantly.
